Offline First

Design for your most constrained environment first&colon; an unreliable network.

A natural extension of Responsive web design and Mobile First thinking, which treat screen size as your primary design constraint, Offline First treats the flakiness of wireless networks as a given. Using the data replication and sync capabilities of Apache CouchDB™, PouchDB, and IBM Cloudant, we’ll show you how to build Offline First principles into your apps so they’re not only highly available, but load faster and perform better too.
